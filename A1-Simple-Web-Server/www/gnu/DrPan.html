Projects (Summary and Posters)

  • Protocols for advanced networking: design and evaluation of link, network and transport layer protocols to support multi-party, multimedia traffic over mobile, wireless networks of various scales
    • Cognitive and Cooperative Networking (CCN)
      • Channel Assignment (CA) for Robust Topology Control (RTC) with Multi-Channel and Multi-Radio (MCMR)
    • Wireless and Mobile Sensor Networks (W/MSN)
    • Vehicular Ad-hoc Networks (VANET), Mobility and Mobile Social Networks (MSN)
  • Performance analysis of networked systems: performance measurement, analysis, diagnosis and improvement of large-scale networked systems in new, challenging environments
    • IPTV service provisioning and in-home distribution
      • Video streaming over multi-hop wireless networks
      • Video streaming over multi-link "no-new-wires" networks
      • Video streaming over UWB networks
    • Large-scale distributed systems (Web, CDN, DNS, BGP, LFN, etc)
    • Peer-to-Peer (P2P) systems
      • File Sharing and Video Streaming
  • Applied network security: enhancing network security, especially on the control plane of the protocol stack, in a practical, affordable way
    • Distributed network measurement
      • P2P Traffic Analysis
    • Network anomaly detection through traffic behavior analysis
      • IEICE 2009 Best Paper Award; TAF 2010 Telesys Award
    • SIP, DNS and BGP security
